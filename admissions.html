Admissions 2026

Begin your academic journey with Jamia Millia Islamia.

Admission Overview

Jamia Millia Islamia offers admission to Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Diploma, Certificate and Doctoral programs.

Admissions are based on entrance examinations, merit, and eligibility criteria depending on the course.

Students can apply online through the Smart Digital Campus portal.

Admission Process

Step 1

Create your account on the admission portal.

Step 2

Fill out the online application form.

Step 3

Upload all required documents.

Step 4

Pay the application fee online.

Step 5

Appear for the entrance examination (if applicable).

Step 6

Check the merit list and complete admission.

Eligibility Criteria

🎓 Undergraduate

Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board.

📚 Postgraduate

Candidates must possess a Bachelor's Degree in the relevant discipline.

📜 Diploma

Candidates should have passed 10+2 or equivalent qualification.

💻 Certificate

Eligibility varies according to the selected certificate course.

Required Documents

📄 Class 10 Marksheet

Self-attested copy.

📄 Class 12 Marksheet

For UG admissions.

🎓 Graduation Degree

Required for PG admissions.

🪪 Identity Proof

Aadhaar Card / Passport / Voter ID.

🖼 Passport Size Photos

Recent coloured photographs.

✍ Signature

Scanned signature for online application.
