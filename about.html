About Jamia Millia Islamia

Building Futures Through Education Since 1920

About the University

Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) is one of India's premier Central Universities, established in 1920. The university is known for its excellence in education, research, innovation, and cultural diversity.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, certificate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines. JMI focuses on holistic development by combining academic excellence, research opportunities, and extracurricular activities.

This Smart Digital Campus website is designed to provide students, faculty members, and visitors with easy access to university information, admission details, academic resources, and campus services.

History

Jamia Millia Islamia was founded in Aligarh in 1920 during the Indian freedom movement by a group of visionary national leaders. Later, the university shifted to New Delhi and was declared a Central University by an Act of Parliament in 1988. Today, JMI is recognized nationally and internationally for its quality education, research contributions, and inclusive learning environment.
